Further Considerations

Through this visual interpretation, my goal was to solidify the correlation between education and poverty levels. However, with education, there is many levels of nuance that must be examined, such ash the quality of education, size of classroom, public vs. private education As all these factors play a role into determining how successful one may become after their education. In addition, no two states, counties, or cities are the same. Each have their unique set of opportunities and challenges, thus making this issue a fascinating one at each level of government. While I wanted to shed light on this topic, my hope is that I can further take part in diving deeper into this issue and using tools like geojson and html to continue creating visual graphics to alert attention to this issue.
